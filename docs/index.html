screenshots

Scan Interface

The scan interface is devided in two sections. On the left side you can run a new scan with preconfigured parameters or your own nmap fu. You have also the possability to upload existing nmap scans created with the -oX parameter.

Scan Interface

On the right side you will see your running and finished scans.

Groups

TThe group interface is the heart of envizon. You can select, group, order, quick search, global search, move, copy, delete and view your clients. The left side represents the group list. If you click on a group you will get a detailed view in the center of the page with the group content. Each client in a group has a link. By clicking on the ip address you will get a more detailed view in the right side with all attributes, labels, ports and nmap output.

Global search

In this section you can search by nearly anything in the database and combining each search parameter with 'AND', 'OR' & 'NOT'.
Perform simple queries for hostname, IP, open ports, etc. or build combined queries like: hostname contains 'win' and mac address starts with '0E:5C' or has port 21 and 22 open.
